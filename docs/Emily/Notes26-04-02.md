## ⚖️ Why your earlier multi-image attempt drifted

When you used multiple images from another model, you likely had:

Even if they looked like “the same person” to you…

and averaging faces = **drift**

You switched to:

👉 **single strong identity → controlled variation**

That gives you:

* a **fixed anchor (this image)**

* consistency across expressions

This is exactly how high-quality LoRAs are built.

---

## 🌿 The everyday core (what most young women actually wear)

### 1. **Cotton (the default)**

* T-shirts, fitted tops, casual wear
* Breathable, easy, familiar

👉 This is your **baseline realism fabric**

Sub-variants that matter:

* **Cotton jersey** → soft, slightly stretchy (most tees)
* **Ribbed cotton** → more form-fitting, slightly structured
* **Cotton blends** → often smoother, holds shape better

---

### 2. **Cotton-elastane blends**

* Cotton + a bit of stretch (2–5%)

👉 This is *hugely* common

Why it matters:

* conforms to the body
* moves with posture
* subtly signals awareness of fit

This is probably what you’re seeing in your generated images.

---

### 3. **Linen (but… selectively)**

* Breathable, textured, wrinkles easily

👉 More “intentional” than cotton

Reads as:

* relaxed confidence
* slightly elevated taste
* warm-weather practicality

Not everyday for everyone — but very “Wardrobe-adjacent” in tone.

---

## 🌸 The slightly more “considered” fabrics

### 4. **Viscose / Rayon**

* Soft, drapey, cool to the touch

👉 Moves differently than cotton — more *flow*

Reads as:

* feminine
* fluid
* a bit more styled

Great for:

* blouses
* soft tops
* anything that *falls* rather than clings

---

### 5. **Modal / Tencel (Lyocell)**

* Very soft, almost silky but casual

👉 Quiet luxury without looking like you tried

Reads as:

* comfort + quality
* subtle self-care

These are increasingly common in nicer basics.

---

## 🌿 The structure vs softness spectrum (very useful for character)

Think of tops like this:

| Fabric Type   | How it behaves         | What it says           |
| ------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Ribbed cotton | Holds shape            | grounded, practical    |
| Cotton jersey | Soft, neutral          | everyday ease          |
| Linen         | Structured but relaxed | intentional simplicity |
| Viscose       | Flowy                  | softness, openness     |
| Modal/Tencel  | Smooth, refined        | quiet confidence       |
