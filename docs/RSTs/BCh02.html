Ted’s Education Begins

French Lesson

Dinner over, the girls repaired to their rooms. In a matter of moments, Ted was left alone with the taciturn Pauline who had busied herself clearing the dishes and generally cleaning up. Presently, she swept close to him, and settled herself on the bench.

“Il parait que vous parlez francais?” she asked gently. (it appears you speak french?)

Ted nodded. “Un peu, oui, mais pas bien,” he stammered shyly. (a little, yes, but not well)
