Context Summary — Celeste’s Girl (Current State + Next-Scene Direction)¶

Where we are now (big picture)¶

  • The story remains elegant, grounded, non-prurient, with a women-led competence network as the moral and structural spine.
  • The atelier’s culture is standards + process + safety, not “rescue,” not drama.

  • Lauren: the adult protector who avoids helicoptering by designing guardrails (protocols, opt-out neutrality) and supplying legitimacy/logistics.
